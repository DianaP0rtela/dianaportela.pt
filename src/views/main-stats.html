Storytelling across Time and Space

Since early age I've been passionate about the world, always looking for different experiences around the corner.
Here you have some curious things about me.

When I was younger I used to be a swimmer and sports always played a key role in my life. A small plot twist, among many others, I've danced 6 different dance styles, and won the 4th place at European Roxy Snowgirls Championship. I have travelled in all 5 continents, visiting 46 different countries, both hemispheres, and backpacking in both North and South America and Australia. While living most of my time in Portugal, I've also soon taken up the experience to study and work abroad. Until now I was living abroad in 10 different cities from 3 continents. I am an icy dip enthusiast, which means I might say that I go for a swim at the ocean around 300 days a year (out of rainy days, of course!). My passion about nature and the sea is ancient! When I was 15 years old I tryied to raise money from my bricolage portfolio to buy a trailer to live in front of the sea! Back in the days, I used to play with bricolage, ceramics, oils and other paintings. Later on, a glimpse of interest in photography led me to win 1st prize at EIT Health Alumni Summit Photography competition and the 2nd place at an university competition.
